About

I recently graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering and a minor in Healthcare Markets.

I’m passionate about translational and interdisciplinary research, particularly in developing medical devices that support personalized medicine and leverage technology to combat disease. I’m especially interested in innovations that address chronic conditions and mental health — areas where long-term solutions can significantly improve quality of life. I'm drawn to projects that span the full device pipeline, from problem identification and design to testing and iteration.
